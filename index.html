About

About

Team Omega

is the student coding club of Presidency University,Bangalore. Where we conduct coding activites to improve the coding culture in the campus and also participate in hackathons to find new innovative solutions to the real world problems.

Vision

To encourage the students to take part in routine coding activities and attend hackathons to improve their coding knowledge and understand their domain.
